The beginning of the story...

Oquirrh Mountain Art was born out of a young boy's love and appreciation of nature and its beauty. This love was nutured over time and began to manifest itself in the drawings of Brett as a young artist. His ability to capture the beauty of the world around him grew as he did. The mediums that the artwork came in grew with time. He now has an extensive portfolio of artwork that is just waiting to grace your living spaces with its serenity...
